# Community

## Purpose

The community directory catalogs free external resources that hackathon participants can use in their projects — APIs, hosting platforms, databases, design tools, datasets, and AI tools.

## Responsibility

This directory curates and maintains lists of free, high-quality resources that make hackathon development accessible to everyone.

## What belongs here

- Free APIs catalog
- Free hosting platforms catalog
- Free databases catalog
- Free design resources catalog
- Free datasets catalog
- Free AI tools catalog
- Community contribution guides

## What does NOT belong here

- AI model information (belongs in `resources/ai/`)
- Integration guides (belongs in `resources/integrations/`)
- Example projects (belongs in `resources/examples/`)

## Future roadmap

This directory will be populated with curated resource lists that are verified, categorized, and maintained by the community.
