# Company

## Purpose

The company directory represents the internal structure of the Hackathon Foundation software company. Everything related to employees, standards, policies, and internal operations belongs here.

## Responsibility

This directory is the organization itself. It contains the shared knowledge, department structure, policies, reusable resources, and onboarding materials that define how the company operates.

## What belongs here

- `handbook/` — Company-wide shared knowledge and engineering standards
- `departments/` — Engineering department and role definitions
- `policies/` — Company policies, rules, and quality gates
- `assets/` — Reusable internal capabilities (templates, skills, workflows)
- `onboarding/` — Guides for new users and AI assistants

## What does NOT belong here

- Public documentation (belongs in `docs/`)
- Public resources like AI model catalogs (belongs in `resources/`)
- Utility scripts (belongs in `scripts/`)

## Future roadmap

This directory will be populated in phases:

1. `handbook/` — Shared context, engineering standards, coding conventions
2. `departments/` — Engineering roles and department definitions
3. `policies/` — Rules, quality gates, and engineering policies
4. `assets/` — Reusable templates, skills, workflows, checklists, playbooks
5. `onboarding/` — Quick-start guides for new users and AI assistants
