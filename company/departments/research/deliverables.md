# Research — Deliverables

## Primary deliverables

### Technology evaluations

Systematic assessments of technologies under consideration. Each evaluation includes:

- Technology description and purpose
- Evaluation criteria and methodology
- Alternatives compared with pros and cons
- Recommendation with rationale
- Free Forever compliance assessment

### Proof-of-concept prototypes

Small-scale implementations that validate technology choices. Includes:

- Working prototype or integration demo
- Implementation notes and challenges encountered
- Performance and usability observations
- Migration considerations for production adoption

### Research reports

In-depth investigations into specific topics. Includes:

- Problem statement and research questions
- Methodology and sources
- Findings and analysis
- Recommendations for action
- References and further reading

### Technology watch

Ongoing awareness of relevant technology developments. Includes:

- Summaries of new tools, frameworks, and releases
- Relevance assessment for the company
- Recommendations for further investigation
- Quarterly technology landscape updates

### Migration guides

Step-by-step plans for transitioning between technologies. Includes:

- Current state and target state
- Migration steps in dependency order
- Risk assessment and rollback plan
- Timeline and resource estimates

## Deliverable quality gate

Every deliverable must pass these checks before leaving Research:

- [ ] Evidence-based — recommendations are supported by systematic evaluation
- [ ] Free Forever compliant — no recommended tool requires payment
- [ ] Actionable — reader knows what to do with the information
- [ ] Referenced — sources and methodology are documented
- [ ] Risk-aware — limitations and risks are disclosed
