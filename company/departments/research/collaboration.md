# Research — Collaboration

## Cross-department workflow

### Engineering → Research

**Handoff:** Engineering provides technology needs, pain points, architecture constraints, and candidate evaluation criteria.

**Research returns:** Technology evaluations, proof-of-concept implementations, migration recommendations.

**Check:** Engineering evaluates research output against architecture principles and decides whether to adopt.

### Design → Research

**Handoff:** Design provides design tooling requirements, accessibility standards needs, and UX research questions.

**Research returns:** Design tool evaluations, accessibility tool recommendations, UX methodology research.

**Check:** Design evaluates research recommendations against design workflow requirements.

### Documentation → Research

**Handoff:** Documentation provides documentation tooling needs, content management requirements.

**Research returns:** Documentation platform evaluations, authoring tool recommendations.

**Check:** Documentation evaluates research recommendations against documentation standards.

### Quality Assurance → Research

**Handoff:** QA provides testing tool needs, automation requirements, and quality methodology questions.

**Research returns:** Testing tool evaluations, automation framework recommendations, QA methodology research.

**Check:** QA evaluates research recommendations against testing strategy and quality goals.

### Operations → Research

**Handoff:** Operations provides infrastructure tool needs, CI/CD requirements, and operational challenges.

**Research returns:** Infrastructure tool evaluations, deployment tool recommendations, operational best practices.

**Check:** Operations evaluates research recommendations against reliability and cost requirements.

## Escalation path

| Issue | Escalate to |
|---|---|
| Unclear research requirements | Research Lead |
| Cross-department conflict | Department leads meeting |
| Recommendation dispute | Research Lead presents evidence; CEO decides |
| Free Forever policy conflict | CEO |
