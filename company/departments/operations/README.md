# Operations

## Purpose

Operations is responsible for the infrastructure, tooling, and processes that enable the development team to build, test, deploy, and maintain the product reliably.

## Scope

- CI/CD pipelines and deployment automation
- Development infrastructure and tooling
- Monitoring, logging, and alerting
- Security and compliance infrastructure
- Environment management
