# Design — Collaboration

## Cross-department workflow

### Engineering → Design

**Handoff:** Engineering provides feasibility feedback, implementation constraints, and technical recommendations.

**Design returns:** Updated designs that account for technical constraints while maintaining quality.

**Check:** Design reviews Engineering's implementation to verify visual and interaction fidelity.

### Documentation → Design

**Handoff:** Documentation provides content structure, language patterns, and text requirements.

**Design returns:** Layouts that accommodate content needs, with spacing and hierarchy for various text lengths.

**Check:** Design verifies that layouts display documentation content correctly across breakpoints.

### Quality Assurance → Design

**Handoff:** QA provides usability testing results, accessibility audit findings, and visual regression reports.

**Design returns:** Design revisions addressing usability and accessibility issues.

**Check:** Design reviews QA findings and incorporates fixes into the design system when patterns emerge.

### Operations → Design

**Handoff:** Operations provides design tooling, asset delivery pipelines, and environment access.

**Design returns:** Design assets in the required formats, with versioned exports.

**Check:** Design verifies that asset delivery pipelines work correctly for Engineering consumption.

### Research → Design

**Handoff:** Research provides UX pattern research, accessibility guidelines, and competitor analysis.

**Design returns:** Design decisions informed by research findings.

**Check:** Design documents how research influenced design decisions.

## Escalation path

| Issue | Escalate to |
|---|---|
| Unclear design requirements | Design Lead |
| Cross-department conflict | Department leads meeting |
| Design system conflict | Design Lead |
| Accessibility issue | CEO |
