# Departments

## Purpose

The departments directory defines the engineering departments and roles within the company. Each department represents a functional area with distinct responsibilities.

## Responsibility

This directory organizes the company into departments and defines the roles within each department. It is the organizational chart of the company.

## What belongs here

- Department directories (Engineering, Design, Testing, Documentation, Operations, Management)
- Role definitions within each department
- Department-specific rules and standards
- Inter-department interaction guidelines

## What does NOT belong here

- Shared company knowledge (belongs in `company/handbook/`)
- Company-wide policies (belongs in `company/policies/`)
- Reusable skills (belongs in `company/assets/`)

## Future roadmap

This directory will be populated with departments and roles defined in `docs/DEPARTMENTS.md` and `docs/ENGINEERING_ROLES.md`.
