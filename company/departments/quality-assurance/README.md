# Quality Assurance

## Purpose

Quality Assurance is responsible for ensuring the product meets defined quality standards. It establishes testing practices, defines quality gates, and prevents defects from reaching users.

## Scope

- Testing strategy and methodology
- Quality standards and metrics
- Test automation
- Bug tracking and reporting
- Release quality gates
