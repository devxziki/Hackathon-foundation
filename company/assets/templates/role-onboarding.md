# Role Onboarding

## Purpose

Standard onboarding template for initializing a new AI assistant into a specific role. Provides all context required to begin productive work.

---

## Step 1: Read the handbook

Read these handbook documents in order:

- `company/handbook/mission.md` — why the project exists
- `company/handbook/company-culture.md` — values and operating principles
- `company/handbook/communication.md` — how to communicate with the CEO
- `company/handbook/engineering-principles.md` — technical standards
- `company/handbook/definition-of-done.md` — quality criteria for all output
- `company/handbook/repository-philosophy.md` — why the repository is structured this way
- `company/handbook/architecture-overview.md` — how the layers connect

## Step 2: Read your role definition

- `company/roles/<department>/<role>.md`

Identify your:
- Core responsibilities
- Inputs and outputs
- Decision scope
- Collaboration map
- Success criteria

## Step 3: Read your department definition

- `company/departments/<department>/README.md`
- `company/departments/<department>/mission.md`
- `company/departments/<department>/responsibilities.md`
- `company/departments/<department>/collaboration.md`

Understand how your department interacts with others.

## Step 4: Read the relevant policies

- `company/policies/execution-policy.md` — mandatory lifecycle
- `company/policies/communication-policy.md` — reporting and blocker rules
- `company/policies/review-policy.md` — review process and gates

Read additional policies specific to your role as listed in your role definition.

## Step 5: Read the current state

- `company/workspace/state/current-task.md` — what is being worked on now
- `company/workspace/state/last-action.md` — what was just done
- `company/workspace/state/progress-snapshot.md` — overall project status
- `company/workspace/state/task-history.md` — completed work

## Step 6: Read the resume protocol

- `company/workspace/handoff/resume-protocol.md` — how to resume work in any tool

## Step 7: Confirm readiness

Confirm the following:

- [ ] I have read the handbook documents in Step 1
- [ ] I understand my role responsibilities (Step 2)
- [ ] I understand my department and its collaborators (Step 3)
- [ ] I know which policies apply to my work (Step 4)
- [ ] I know the current task and last action (Step 5)
- [ ] I know how to resume after a break (Step 6)

## Next step

Begin work on the task described in `company/workspace/state/current-task.md`. If no task is assigned, notify the CEO.
